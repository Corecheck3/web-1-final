“I want to give people the opportunity to look at the world through a deeper and more creative lens.”


My name is Megan Lasso. I am a Design Major and have about a year until I graduate with a degree. I specialize in illustrating logos and designing the structure of new products that will help make people's lives easier.

Images of my work:
-The “B Rose”, or rose, illustration represents my older sister and her middle name.
-The Lake Wood logo, or “Wordmark”, was made for a restaurant.
-The tree, or “Hope” illustration was made for packaging for a company and it represents hope.
-The panel named “Comical”, was drawn for a futuristic comic strip.
-The “Summer” logo was made to promote summer, though now that I look at it, I would change the colors.
